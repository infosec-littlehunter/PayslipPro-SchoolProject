Calculation Framework

Understand each component of your payroll calculation process

Base Salary

Starting point for all calculations

Your base salary is the fundamental component. It's determined by your employment contract and forms the foundation for all payroll calculations, including overtime and additional compensation.

  • Monthly salary as per employment contract
  • Hourly rates automatically converted to monthly equivalent
  • Overtime rates (1.5x-2x) calculated separately from base
  • Prorated for part-time employees and mid-month joiners

Allowances

Additional compensations and benefits

Allowances are supplementary payments provided by employers. They are added to your base salary to form your gross salary and typically receive the same tax treatment as salary income.

  • Housing allowance for accommodation support
  • Transportation allowance for commute costs
  • Meal allowance covering daily meal expenses
  • Performance bonuses based on KPI achievement

Deductions

Mandatory and voluntary reductions

Deductions are amounts subtracted from gross salary. These include mandatory contributions (taxes and social security) and voluntary deductions (insurance, loans, contributions) as per Cambodia regulations.

  • Income tax (IRC) on taxable income above threshold
  • Social security contribution (0.8% employee share)
  • Health insurance premiums for employee coverage
  • Loans and advances repayment per agreement

Cambodia Government Compliance

Full compliance with all Cambodia regulations, tax requirements, and banking standards

Income Tax (IRC)

Progressive taxation managed by payroll system

PayrollPro automatically calculates income tax according to Cambodia General Department of Taxation (DGT) regulations. The system manages progressive tax brackets and ensures accurate withholding at source, with monthly reporting to government authorities.

  • Personal Exemption: 1,000,000 KHR per month threshold
  • Tax Brackets: Progressive rates from 0% to 20% per income level
  • Monthly Reporting: Automatic IRC forms submitted to DGT
  • Withholding Accuracy: Employer withholds and remits on behalf of employee

Social Security (NSSF)

Employee and employer contributions for benefits

National Social Security Fund contributions are mandatory for all employees in Cambodia. PayrollPro accurately calculates and tracks both employee and employer contributions, manages enrollment, and ensures monthly remittance with proper documentation to NSSF authorities.

  • Employee Rate: 0.8% of gross salary deducted
  • Employer Rate: 0.8% of gross salary contributed
  • Coverage: Old age, disability, and survivor benefits included
  • Monthly Remittance: Automatic payments and compliance reporting

Labor Law Standards

Compliance with Cambodia Labor Law requirements

PayrollPro ensures full compliance with Cambodia's Labor Law, including minimum wage requirements, working hour regulations, leave policies, and severance calculations. The system tracks all employment metrics required by law and generates necessary compliance reports.

  • Minimum Wage: Statutory rates enforced per province and year
  • Working Hours: 48 hours per week with overtime tracking
  • Leave Entitlements: Annual, sick, and maternity leave managed automatically
  • Severance Calculation: Accurate computation per Labor Law requirements

Banking Standards

Direct deposit and payment processing standards

PayrollPro integrates seamlessly with Cambodia's major banks including ABA Bank. The system generates bank-compatible payment files, maintains detailed audit trails, performs automatic reconciliation, and ensures secure and compliant salary disbursement to employee accounts.

  • Direct Deposit: ABA Bank and other major banks integrated
  • File Formats: Compatible with ABA ACH and other banking standards
  • Audit Trail: Complete transaction history for compliance verification
  • Reconciliation: Automatic bank and payroll reconciliation tools
